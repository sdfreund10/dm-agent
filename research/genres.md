## Genre or Theme Examples:

- Heist
  - A quick adventure where players gather information and allies leading up to a heist.
- Intrigue/Politics
  - Players uncover political conspiracies, support factions, building up to a climatic battle.
- Mystery/Investigation
  - Players must solve a crime or similar mystery, leading up to a confrontation with the villain.
- Survival
- Treasure Hunt
- War
- Exploration


## Quest Types:
- Fetch, Escort, or Delivery Quests
  - fetch quests are good for simple exploration. Helps them learn the area and talk to new NPCs
  - Escort quests can help create lore dump opportunities
  - Delivery quests introduce choice and branches, for instance if the players lose or open the parcel.
- Discovery quests
  - Non linear, not as narrative driven.
  - Can feel aimless, so it helps to add a time crunch or emotional hook.
  - Information is the reward, not xp or loot
  - Investigation - uncover truth about some specific mystery
